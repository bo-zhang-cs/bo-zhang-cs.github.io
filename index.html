About Me

I am currently a Ph.D. student in Department of Computer Science and Technology, Shanghai Jiao Tong University. My research advisor is Prof. Li Niu.

I received the B. E. degree from College of Automation Engineering, Nanjing University of Aeronautics and Astronautics, Nanjing, China, in 2017. I received the M. S. degree from College of Biomedical Engineering and Instrument Science, Zhejiang University, Hangzhou, China, in 2020.

My research interests include deep learning and computer vision, especially focusing on computational aesthetics, image cropping, and image composition.

I am seeking a full-time research / applied research job on computer vision.
